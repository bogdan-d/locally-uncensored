Local AI Image and Video Generator in One App

Here is the gap nobody talks about: the popular local AI apps are text only. LM Studio, Jan, Ollama, Msty, all excellent for chat, none of them makes a single image. So people install one app for chat, fight through ComfyUI for images, and give up before video entirely. Locally Uncensored is a plug-and-play local AI studio that generates chat, images, and video in one app, on your own GPU, with nothing filtered.

No Node Graphs Required

ComfyUI is the reigning power tool for local image generation, and it earns its reputation, after you learn to wire node graphs. In Locally Uncensored the Create tab is a prompt box, a model picker, and a gallery. Pick FLUX schnell, type a prompt, click generate. The Model Manager downloads image and video models with one click, the same way it handles chat models. If you outgrow the simple path, our ComfyUI beginner guide shows the power-user route.

Uncensored, Because It Is Your Hardware

Every cloud image generator puts a moderation layer between you and the model, and the rules change without notice. On your own GPU there is no such layer: the model does what you ask. That is not a loophole, it is the entire point of local generation. Adult and unfiltered creation stays free and private on your own machine, as covered in uncensored AI video locally.

One App or Three?

You could assemble the same capabilities from parts: LM Studio for chat, ComfyUI for images, a research repo for video. It works, and it costs you three installs, three update cycles, and three learning curves. The studio approach puts the whole pipeline behind one installer, with models managed in one place and outputs in one gallery. The 2026 app comparison shows the landscape side by side.

FAQ

Is there one app for both local images and video?

Yes, Locally Uncensored: FLUX and SD for images, Wan and LTX for video, one installer, one gallery.

What GPU do I need?

8 GB VRAM for comfortable images, 10 to 12 GB for usable video. See the table above.

Do I need ComfyUI knowledge?

No. Prompt box, model picker, generate. ComfyUI remains available as the power-user path.

Is it really uncensored?

On your own hardware, yes: no cloud moderation layer exists between you and the model.
